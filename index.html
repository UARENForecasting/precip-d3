The University of Arizona

This plot shows the cumulative precipitation at TIA for each year. Years are colored by the JAN-FEB MEI ENSO value. Hover your cursor over a line to get a tooltip with exact values. Water year starts on October 1 of the previous year. The plot currently shows Oct-Apr, but it's easy to change it to include the monsoon.
